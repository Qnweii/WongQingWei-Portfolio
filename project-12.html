OVERVIEW

Background

Lynx is an anthropomorphic (human) cat from a parallel universe where she is one of the Empire's strongest warriors. She wields one special leech blade that draws power from her own body to fight off her enemies. Her helmet and outfit are specially designed to help her teleport long distances.

This character was inspired by Blaze the Cat, a character from the "Sonic the Hedgehog" film. For my first character design, I wanted to create a fictional humanized animal because these characters are easier to give off their own personality and story through their style and appearances.

Creating fictional characters gives me many new ideas and helps to develop my creativity as I can learn to exhibit a character's characteristics through the way they are presented.
Lynx was my first character that I designed and created, and I enjoyed the process of researching many different styles to appear like the character I wanted her to be. It was really satisfying to see the character I created come to life!
