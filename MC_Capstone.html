OVERVIEW

Background

This app takes you on a nostalgic and immersive experience of cooking Ang Ku Kueh with your grandmother with Augmented Reality.
Users will firstly have to scan the AR Marker that is placed on a post card. The app will then go through the steps of making Ang Ku Kueh in which the user can follow along. The user can follow the steps by interacting with the different 3D models by clicking, pinching sliding, etc.

This experience is further enhanced through the voice over of the grandmother, sound effects, simple animations and the interaction with the mini kitchen illustrated on the postcard.
After the user is done making the Ang Ku Kueh, the user gets to share their favorite memory when eating Ang Ku Kueh. The user will also get to see responses inputted by others which is achieved with Firebase.
