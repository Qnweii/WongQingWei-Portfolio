Project:

Recreate Helix Jump Marketing Designs

Project Type
Individual project
Project Goals
To recreate the marketing designs for the game, 'Helix Jump'.
Project Task
To recreate and design the app's logo, the splash screen and the infographic poster design.
Tool
Adobe Illustrator
Duration
2 weeks

OVERVIEW

Background

This is the finalized designs of the game ‘Helix Jump’ that I have created. The use of bright colors, mostly different shades of yellow, is to highlight the intensity of the game and the graphics depicts the concept of the game.
I used the colors sparingly and with discretion, mainly orange, yellow, red, and black, so all the colors placed together will complement and balance each other so that the texts and graphics will stand out. Overall, all these design are meant to be simple to complement the nature of the game yet informative as well.
