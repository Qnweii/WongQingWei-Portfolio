OVERVIEW

Background

Skills Bridge SG is an educational pathway planner that helps learners and parents discover, compare, and plan post-secondary and tertiary study options in Singapore.
It generates course information, entry criteria, and personalised future roadmap into a single application for accessibility and convenience.
