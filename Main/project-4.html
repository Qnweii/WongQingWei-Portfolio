OVERVIEW

Background

This was one of my first projects using Unity and is my first exterior environment. The main objective of this project is to achieve a realistic and stylized look, as all my previous work are focusing on interior environment. This project was to challange myself to create a walkable exterior environment with a different mood of mystical and mysterious island.

All the assets were modelled in Maya, textured in Substance Painter and assembled in Unity. Megascan assets were also used for the stone, grass, and statues to create the realistic-looking island, hold with mystery and life.

I also experimented with all the VFX effects that can be seen, which includes the god rays, waterfall, water fountain, lakes and the fireflies to complete the environment with a more realistic touch.
