Project:

2D Illustration Landscape With 3D Interactive Assets

Project Type
Individual project
Project Goals
Design an AR Site embedded with 3D interactive assets.
Project Task
To create quality 2D visual components embedded into the 3D environment and apply relevant digital tools to create, reproduce, and distribute visual design messages.
Tool
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Aero Beta
Duration
2 weeks

OVERVIEW

Background

"It is the time you have wasted for your rose that makes your rose so important."
This design was inspired by the novel called 'The Little Prince'. This scene was meant to convey the astronaut has come a long way and do not wish to return home due to the special connection he has with his companion, the fox, and the rose he had always adored from a distance.

Project Preview


DESIGN RATIONALE

I enjoyed reading 'The Little Prince' since I was young as it was such a beautifully written yet simple story. Therefore, I was inspired to come up with a design that could convey the importance of cherishing the little things in life, even if they seem far-fetched.
One of my favourite quote that also inspired this design: “If you love a flower which happens to be on a star, it is sweet at night to gaze at the sky. All the stars are a riot of flowers.”
