Project Type
Individual project

To showcase modular asset modeling
and texturing skillset for real-time engine production.

Tool
Maya
Substance 3D Painter
Unity
Duration
3 weeks

OVERVIEW

Background

This was one of my first projects using Unity and it was my first time working on a sci-fi themed environment. The main objective of this project, which was completed in 3 weeks, was to create a walkable sci-fi corridor environment and viewed from the first-person perspective.

Since most of the projects I've worked on are medieval theme, this one had a completely different concept, which made it more interesting as I get to explore the different styles and approaches when creating the setting.

Finding many reference images online provided me with many ideas for conceptualizing and designing my own sci-fi corridor environment.

All the assets were modelled in Maya and textured in Substance Painter individually, and I made use of texture atlas and trim sheet to apply to modular assets.

I also played around with the particle effects to complete the environment with a more realistic touch.
