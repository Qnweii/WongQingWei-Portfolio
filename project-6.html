Project:

A Tote Bag Design Contest

Project Type
Individual project
Project Goals
To showcase creativity and originality
by translating injury prevention into a tote bag design
that will be launched during World Trauma Day 2023.
Project Task
Design an illustration that highlights the importance to be aware about Water Safety.
Tool
Adobe Illustrator
Duration
1 week

OVERVIEW

Background

My design uses a newspaper concept to represent the theme of drowning, also known as ‘The Silent Killer’, with scattered articles on actual drowning incidents. As most drowning victims could not show signs for help, I used a geometric pop art style with different shades of blue to illustrate the child, who is drowning, fading into the water like he is invisible, to convey the loneliness and despair he is feeling.

Messy scribbles were used to represent the child’s mental state and the waves of emotions he is experiencing while drowning but still alive. This design highlights how drowning can feel and the importance of being aware that anyone can drown and go unnoticed. This serves as a reminder to all to be vigilant at all times when around bodies of water because drowning can occur silently and lead to death.
